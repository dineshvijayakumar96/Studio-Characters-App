I2C2

I2C2 is a minimalist Framer template created exclusively for The Brand Identity. It is made for designers or studios looking to showcase their work using a simple grid and reduced typography while still impressing with customised on-scroll effects.

Custom interactions, animations and light and dark modes add a touch of personality, while the parallax effect adds depth and dimensionality to the design. In addition to its visual features and built-in CMS, colours and typefaces can be changed in seconds; making it easy to customise and ensuring that your portfolio website looks great on any device.

Framer is a no-code design tool made specifically for creating bespoke websites and shipping them with a custom domain. Publishing a Framer website online requires a paid Framer subscription.
